Tableau Dashboard Documentation
1) Objective

The dashboard was designed to track sales performance, as well as ssess delivery durations of products to customers, and also analyse product review scores

2) Data Source

Source: Transformed dataset from excel (Power Query)

Update Frequency: Static (for project)

3) Dashboard Structure

page 1: revenue analysis

page 2: delivery duration assessment

page 3: product performance

page 4: product review analysis


4) Interactivity

Some sections of the dashboard include a dynamic filter by order status allowing for comparisons between the status of various orders

5) Key Insights Derived

-There was an overall rise in revenue trend until september 2018, which saw a significant drop (99.6%) in revenue from the previous month. However on a YoY basis, revenue was up 20%.

-Averege delivery duration was highest in the northern parts of the country compared to other areas. It was also marinally strongly negatively correlated with number of sellers per state as seen on the scatter plot.

-Most of the products had a review score between 4 and 5 indicating strong customer satisfaction.
